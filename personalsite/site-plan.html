Overview

Purpose

Since my degree is in computer programing, I enjoy programming and building websites. I want to create a website that offers easy access to ordering flowers from a local flower shop. No matter where you’re at, whether you’re at the office or sitting in rush hour traffic. Think of the last time you bought flowers. Did you go to a flower shop, or did you stop at the store? I bet you stopped at the store. That’s where my website would give you the chance to have fresh flowers for your loved ones with just a click of a button.

Audience

The target audience include families, couples, and single people. Whether they are in need of wedding or funeral flowers. Flowers for an anniversary or birthday. A big dance is coming up or maybe even just some last-minute flowers for that date. The website is there to help people that either don’t want to bother going to the flower shop or maybe they just don’t have time. With the website being accessible from your computer at work, your laptop at home or your mobile devices while sitting in traffic they can have access to fresh flower form anywhere.

Content

Home page

For any occasion: We understand that life can be crazy sometimes, and flowers are usually only thought of for special occasions. That's just not true, they are perfect for any occasion, and they are great for any time of year. Whether that's something planned out weeks in advance or something that more last minute. Flowers have the unique ability to brighten both people's moods and environment. We understand that people have busy lives, that's where we come into play by making ordering flowers online easier. We are happy to deliver anywhere in our area with the prices ranging from 5 dollars for local and going up the greater the distance.

Special Occasions

Weddings: When it comes to your wedding day it should be one of the most special days of your life. The flowers should be just as special. Offering a wide of options ranging from boutonnieres and corsages. To the bridal bouquet, a throw bouquet, center pieces for the tables and even for the cake. We offer many different flower options to make your day special. With prices ranging from the in expensive carnations to the always popular rose. Even offering some of more unique options. Let us help make your day even more special. When it comes to weddings, we need at least 2 weeks' notice because they require us to special order them so we can make sure they are exactly what you need for your special day. Mother's Day: Everybody loves their mothers and wants to show their appreciation for them. What better way then getting them a beautiful bouquet of fresh flowers. Whether that's something simple like a dozen roses in a wrap to more complex bouquet. Let us be your choice to for your Mother's Day flowers. Valentine's day: Nothing says you love your significant other on Valentine's Day like fresh flowers. No matter what kind they like we offer it. From lily's, tulips or even roses we have them. Let help make your valentines day even more special for the special someone in your life. We always have fresh flowers on hand, so no matter how busy you are we always have your back for those last-minute flower needs.

Sympathy

Funeral: it's never an easy when you lose a loved one, but a funeral should be celebration of life. There for the flowers should be an extension in every aspect. From the casket mount to the easel even down to the smallest container. If you have any special items like, their favorite sport, a hobby, maybe even something forms their career. Any of these ideas can be incorporated into the arrangements. There are also options for smaller arrangements that are great options to send directly to the family to help brighten up there day during this difficult time. If there is something specific, you would like to say to the family we are happy to add it to the card. Other wise we will add something appropriate to the card. Get well soon: Being in sick is never a fun experience. That's where our flowers come into play, because whether there in the hospital because of a broken bone or something more serious. Or just at home because of the flu or covid-19, there day can still be brightened up with some flowers. Options include simple tasteful wraps, small arrangements, large arrangements. Some even including a stuffed animal so they can have a companion while they are sick. When it comes to get well arrangements the cards that are included. The costumer would provide what is on the card. But we also offer the option to add something simple if you don't provide something for the card.
